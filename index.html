Wildfire Research Notification System

This page allows users to opt in to receive SMS notifications from the Wildfire Research Notification System, operated by the University of Hawaii.

Purpose of Messaging

SMS messages are sent to notify users when a wildfire event is detected. Messages are strictly informational and transactional.

How to Opt In

  1. Enter your mobile phone number in the form below.
  2. Review the SMS consent language.
  3. Manually check the SMS consent checkbox.
  4. Click Submit to complete the opt-in process.

SMS messages are sent only after the user completes all steps above.

Opt-Out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.
