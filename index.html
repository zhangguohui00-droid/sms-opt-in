21st Annual Superintendent Conference

May 14–15, 2026 | Kāneʻohe, Oʻahu

The 21st Annual Superintendent Conference brings together public works and facility maintenance professionals from across Hawaiʻi to share best practices, strengthen partnerships, and explore innovative solutions supporting our island communities.
