Purpose of Messaging

This service sends transactional and informational SMS notifications related to wildfire detection events and system notifications. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

SMS Opt-In Form


How Consent Is Collected

Users opt in exclusively through this publicly accessible web form by entering their mobile phone number and explicitly checking the SMS consent checkbox. Consent is required before any SMS notifications are sent.

Opt-In Confirmation

Consent is recorded prior to sending any SMS messages. Messages are sent only to phone numbers that have explicitly opted in.

Opt-Out Instructions

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Upon receiving a STOP message, no further SMS messages will be sent.

Privacy Policy

Phone numbers are collected solely for the purpose of sending wildfire notification messages. Phone numbers are not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties and are retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.
