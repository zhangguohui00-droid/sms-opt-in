Purpose of Messaging

This service sends SMS notifications to alert users when a wildfire event is triggered, including detection alerts and system notifications.

SMS Opt-In Form


How Consent Is Collected

  • Users explicitly opt in by entering their phone number and confirming consent before enabling SMS alerts during system setup.
  • Written acknowledgment may also be provided by the phone number owner requesting SMS notifications for testing or operational purposes.

Opt-In Confirmation

Consent is recorded prior to sending any SMS messages. Messages are sent only to phone numbers that have explicitly opted in.

Opt-Out Instructions

Users may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Upon receiving a STOP message, no further SMS messages will be sent.
